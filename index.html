About Aarti Steels Ltd

Aarti Steels Ltd is the flagship steel business of the Aarti Group, a diversified industrial house established in 1979 with interests spanning steel, textiles and healthcare. Over four decades, the company has grown into a fully integrated carbon and alloy steel manufacturer, supplying critical long products to the automotive, engineering and components industries across India and beyond.

Heritage & Growth

What began as a single steel unit has matured into an end-to-end operation that controls the full value chain — from steel melting and refining to continuous casting and precision rolling. This integration lets Aarti Steels hold tight control over chemistry, cleanliness and consistency, the three qualities that matter most to demanding alloy-steel customers.

Manufacturing Facilities

🏭

Ludhiana, Punjab

The group's northern hub for alloy and carbon steel production, serving the dense automotive and components belt of North India.

Quality & Integration

Because melting, casting and rolling sit under one roof, every heat can be traced and controlled — supported by spectrometers, mechanical-testing labs and quality systems. The result is alloy steel that meets the close tolerances and metallurgical properties that automotive and engineering customers depend on.

Why Aarti TalentForge

A plant this integrated runs on a remarkably wide range of skills — a Rolling Mill engineer, a Power Plant operator, a SAP consultant, a Cyber Security analyst and a Finance professional share almost nothing on paper. Aarti TalentForge gives each function its own AI scoring profile, so every applicant is judged against the work that actually happens in that department, helping the HR team move the right people to interview faster and more objectively.

Note: Company information here is indicative and compiled for this internal HR tool. Resume scoring is heuristic and built to assist — not replace — human judgement. All processing happens locally in your browser; candidate data is stored only on this device.
