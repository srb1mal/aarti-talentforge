About Aarti TalentForge

Aarti TalentForge is the in-house AI resume intelligence platform of Aarti Steels Ltd. Part of the Aarti Group established in 1979, Aarti Steels is a fully integrated carbon and alloy steel manufacturer with state-of-the-art plants in Ludhiana (Punjab) and Cuttack (Odisha), producing alloy steel rounds & squares, spring steel, tyre bead wire and specialised wires for the automotive and engineering industries.

A steel plant runs on dozens of very different skill sets — a Rolling Mill engineer, a Power Plant operator, a SAP consultant and a Cyber Security analyst have almost nothing in common on paper. TalentForge gives each function its own AI scoring profile so every resume is judged against the work that actually happens in that department.
